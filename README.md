# **Zombie Blaster** 🎮

**Zombie Blaster** is a fast-paced, arcade-style 2D shoot'em up game inspired by classic titles like *Chicken Invaders*. Developed using **Godot Engine** with **C#**, this project is both a nostalgic homage and a modernized gameplay experience.

---

## 🧩 **Game Concept**
Take control of a spaceship at the bottom of the screen and defend the universe from alien zombie. Progress through waves of enemies, achieve your best score, and challenge the ultimate boss in the climactic final level.

---

## 🎯 **Player Objective**
- Destroy all alien zombie waves.
- Defy the odds to reach **Level 10** and eliminate the **final boss**.
- Survive as long as possible while scoring the highest!

---

## 🕹️ **Controls**
- **Move Left:** Arrow Left / A  
- **Move Right:** Arrow Right / D  
- **Shoot:** Spacebar  
*Note: Movement is horizontal only.*

---

## 🚀 **Core Gameplay**
### Player:
- **Position:** Fixed at bottom of screen.
- **Actions:**
  - Horizontal movement only.
  - Shoots projectiles upwards.
- **Loss Conditions:**
  - Collision with an enemy or enemy projectile.

### Enemies (Zombie):
- Spawn in organized rows with randomized movement.
- Gradually descend towards the player while shooting projectiles.

### **Boss Encounter** – Level 10:
- A formidable foe with unique attack patterns and high health points.
- Features elaborate projectile-based attacks.

---

## 🧱 **Levels & Difficulty**
- **Progressive Challenge:** 10 levels. Enemies evolve with higher speeds and firing rates.  
- **Level 10 Boss Fight:** A dramatic final challenge awaits players.

---

## ⭐ **Scoring System**
- **Goal:** Maximize your score before death or victory.
- **Scoring Examples:**
  - Single Enemy: **+10 points**.
  - Boss Hit: **+Varies (X points)**.

---

## ⬆️ **Gameplay Upgrades**
- Triggered at set score milestones or pre-defined moments:
  - **Power-Ups Include:**
    - Faster shooting.
    - Double projectiles.
    - Higher damage.
    - Pass-through damage
    - Improved player movement speed.
- **Note:** Upgrades apply to the current session – no progression carryover between games.

---

## 💀 **Game End & Restart**
- **Game Over Triggers:**
  - Death of the player.
  - Completion of all levels.
- **Post-Game Options:**
  - Display final score.
  - Return to the main menu.

---

## 🏠 **Game Screens**
1. **Home Screen:** Last score, play button.
2. **Game Screen:** Displays HUD (score, level).
3. **Game Over Screen:** Shows final score, return button.

---

## 🎨 **Art Direction**
- Minimalistic 2D visuals with a cartoon/arcade aesthetic.
- Lightweight animations for retro appeal.

---

## 🔊 **Audio Design**
- Classic chiptune-inspired **arcade music**.  
- SFX for shooting, explosions, upgrades, and boss actions.

---

## 🛠️ **Technical Overview**
- **Engine:** Godot.

- Simple, maintainable design.
